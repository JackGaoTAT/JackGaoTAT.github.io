Console game

Console games, formerly known as console games, also known as video games, include handheld games and console games. It is an interactive multimedia for entertainment. Usually refers to the use of a TV screen as a display, and home console games are executed on the TV.

Game Genres, commonly used to categorize video games, are a way to tell the difference between games. There is no uniform classification method in the world. Game types are mainly divided into 6 categories: action, adventure, simulation, role-playing, casual and others, each of which has dozens of branches, forming a huge "game type tree".

After 2009, a new type of game appeared, called sandbox game, which usually contains four types of elements: action, adventure, survival, and role-playing.
