Resident evil 2

 

"Resident Evil 2: Remake (BIOHAZARD RE: 2)" is one of the works of the famous Japanese survival horror video game "Resident Evil" series. It is a remake of the 1998 game "Resident Evil 2". issued. It will be launched on PlayStation 4, Xbox One and PC platforms, built using the same engine RE Engine as "Resident Evil 7", and the classic survival horror game is officially reborn.

The remake of "Resident Evil 2" will also be developed and produced by Hirabayashi, the producer of "Resident Evil HD Remake". This time, "Resident Evil 2" will be a complete remake. The word used is "Remake" , not "Remaster". This remake of Raccoon City will be completely redone. The storyline is divided into two plot lines: the Lyon line (Leon Scott Kennedy) and the Claire Redfield line. Leon S. Kennedy hadn't heard from his new position for days, so he decided to drive to Raccoon City to find out. But what he didn't know was that there was a nightmare waiting for him ahead. Claire Redfield was about to face another nightmare, and she didn't know how drastically her ordinary life would change...
