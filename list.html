Sekiro

Sekiro is a third-person action role-playing sandbox game where the player takes control of a ninja to save his master: a young Miko who inherits an ancient lineage, and take revenge on his enemies. Players need to use prosthetic hands to equip various deadly weapons, show off their ninja skills, and confront life-threatening enemies in a dark, twisted world.

Resident Evil 2

The characters and backgrounds are based on the original work, and they are re-examined from the beginning. This is the beginning of a new "horror tragedy" that is full of nostalgia and different from the previous one.
